Indemnity Girls

We spent the day together
at the Fidelity International Building,
dragging romance into its furthest minus.
The sensation of a never breaking wave
in a lower abdominal world of debt.
You can live without your life no problem,
as long as you have access to
Indemnity Girls.
You can have as many as stones can fill a beach,
each girl a grey policy
for the coin trapped in the throat.
